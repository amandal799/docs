## What is Acadine?

Acadine is the **operating system for educational institutes**—an AI-powered platform that evaluates handwritten answer sheets, generates question papers, and unlocks performance insights across every student, batch, and center you manage.

Stop spending weekends grading papers and nights creating question papers. Focus on what matters: teaching and growing your institute.

## How It Works

Acadine follows a simple workflow that powers your entire institute:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Setup Your Institute">
    Onboard your centers, create courses aligned to exam boards, and set up batches for different programs.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Manage Users">
    Add students, teachers, and admins. Assign them to appropriate batches and centers with role-based access.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create & Schedule Assessments">
    Use AI to generate question papers in minutes. Schedule tests and notify students automatically.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Evaluate & Share Results">
    Upload handwritten answer sheets. Our AI evaluates them in 24 hours with detailed feedback. Share results instantly.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Analyze & Improve">
    Access performance insights at every level—student, batch, subject, topic, and center. Identify weak areas and take action.
  </Step>
</Steps>
