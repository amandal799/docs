## Welcome, Student!

As a **Student** on Acadine, you'll access study materials, take tests, complete assignments, and track your performance—all in one place. Get detailed feedback on every test and watch your progress over time.

<Info>
**Your learning journey, tracked and optimized**

Acadine provides instant access to study materials, automated test evaluations with detailed feedback, and comprehensive performance analytics to help you improve.
</Info>

## Getting Access to Acadine

There are two ways you'll get access:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Onboarded by Admin">
    Your institute admin adds you directly with your details.

    **You'll receive:**
    - Confirmation email with login credentials
    - Immediate access to your dashboard
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Sign Up via Invite Link">
    Your admin shares an invite link for your batch. You sign up and submit your details.

    **Next steps:**
    - Admin reviews and approves your request
    - You receive a confirmation email once approved
    - You can then log in and access your dashboard
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## What You Can Do on Acadine

### Study Materials

Access all learning resources uploaded by your teachers—PDFs, videos, notes, and presentations.

**Browse by:**
- Subject
- Chapter
- Topic

**Access anytime:**
- Through your dashboard
- Via mobile app

<Card title="Access Study Materials" icon="book-open" href="/study-materials/upload-study-material">
  View and download study materials
</Card>

### Calendar & Events

View all your upcoming lectures and tests in one place.

**Features:**
- Calendar view of all scheduled events
- Lecture details (date, time, location/link for online classes)
- Test schedules with automatic notifications

**Notifications:**
- Mobile app push notifications (primary)
- Email alerts (if mobile app not installed)

### Assignments

Complete practice work and homework assigned by your teachers.

**How it works:**
1. View active assignments on your dashboard
2. Complete the assignment offline
3. Upload your handwritten answer sheet
4. Teacher may manually grade (assignments are NOT auto-evaluated)

<Card title="Upload Assignment Answer Sheet" icon="file-arrow-up" href="/assignments/upload-answer-sheet">
  Learn how to submit assignments
</Card>

<Note>
**Assignment vs Test**

- **Assignment:** Practice/homework → You upload answer sheets → Optional manual grading by teacher
- **Test:** Formal assessment → Admin uploads answer sheets → Auto-evaluated by AI in 24 hours
</Note>

### Tests

Tests are formal assessments conducted offline (usually in the presence of an invigilator).

**How it works:**
1. **Scheduled:** Teachers schedule tests, and you receive notifications
2. **Take the test:** Complete the test offline on paper
3. **Admin uploads:** After the test, admin scans and uploads all answer sheets
4. **AI evaluates:** Acadine's AI evaluates your answers in 24 hours
5. **Results published:** You receive detailed feedback and results

**What you get:**
- Detailed feedback for every question
- Step-wise marking showing where you earned or lost marks
- Rubric-based evaluation aligned to exam board standards (CBSE, ICSE, UPSC, etc.)

<Card title="View Test Schedules" icon="calendar-check" href="/tests-evaluation/schedule-test">
  See your upcoming tests
</Card>

### View Results & Challenge Grades

Once tests are evaluated, you get instant access to your results with comprehensive feedback.

<Steps>
  <Step title="View Your Results">
    Access detailed test results including:
    - Overall score and percentage
    - Question-wise marks breakdown
    - Detailed feedback for each answer
    - Correct answers and marking scheme
  </Step>

  <Step title="Challenge Grades (If Needed)">
    **Don't agree with your score?** You can challenge the evaluation.

    **How it works:**
    1. Submit a grade challenge with your reason
    2. System automatically reviews the evaluation
    3. Teacher may manually review if needed
    4. Updated grade is communicated to you

    This ensures fair, transparent evaluation and gives you a voice in the process.

    <Card title="Challenge a Grade" icon="scale-balanced" href="/tests-evaluation/challenge-grade">
      Learn how to dispute test scores
    </Card>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
**Grade challenges are important!**

If you genuinely believe your answer was evaluated incorrectly, always submit a challenge. The system is designed to ensure fair grading, and your feedback helps improve accuracy.
</Warning>

**You'll see:**

- Course or center-related information

**Notifications:**
- Mobile app alerts
- Email (if app not installed)

## Understanding Your Performance

Acadine provides comprehensive analytics to help you understand exactly where you stand and where to improve.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Tests Over Time" icon="chart-line">
    Track your test performance over weeks and months.

    **See:**
    - Number of tests taken over time
    - Score trends (improving, declining, or stable)
    - Performance patterns

    Visualize your progress and stay motivated.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Average Score Trends" icon="chart-area">
    Monitor how your average score changes over time.

    **See:**
    - Overall average score across all tests
    - Subject-wise average trends
    - Comparison with previous periods

    Identify if you're improving or need to focus more.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Subject-Wise Performance" icon="flask">
    Drill down into performance by subject, chapter, and topic.

    **Drill-down capability:**
    - Start with subject-level performance (e.g., Physics, Chemistry, Math)
    - Click to see chapter-level mastery
    - Drill further into topic and subtopic performance

    **See exactly:**
    - Which subjects you're strong in
    - Which chapters need more focus
    - Specific topics where you struggle

    Use this to guide your study priorities.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Summary Statistics" icon="calculator">
    Quick stats to benchmark your performance:

    - **Total tests taken** - How many assessments you've completed
    - **Current rank** - Your position in the batch
    - **Average score per test** - Overall performance metric

    These give you an instant snapshot of where you stand.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Info>
**All analytics update automatically in real-time**

As soon as test results are published, your analytics are instantly updated. No manual refresh needed.
</Info>
